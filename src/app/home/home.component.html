What does Article 21 guarantee?

Constitution of India

Article 21 - Protection of life and personal liberty

No person shall be deprived of life or personal liberty except according to procedure established by law.

AI Summary

Article 21 protects life, dignity and personal freedom, and the State can limit it only through valid legal procedure.
